Legend of Cards is an exclusive playing card club comprized of Cardslinger NFTs that are driven by the poker community—to create a network of like-minded degens, elevate the brand to Web3 and soon, own a place in the metaverse.

It's 2046, and the world as we know it is dead.

The Authority, who once swore to protect humanity, has traded the people's hope for total global control. They took away everything, including all playing cards. Obey, or risk a precarious fate!

But from the depths of The Basement, a place where like-minded D-genz gather, rises a collective of Cardslingers. Some just for the love of playing poker, while some are called to greater things.

There's an urban legend of an OG Cardslinger who's discovered that poker rules contain the secret to bringing down The Authority. Now, he's assembling a gang of Cardslingers to decipher this secret and create a free, decentralized world. Are you one of us, or one of them?

Stay tuned for more.

Cardslingers are a collection NFTs - unique digital poker-themed collectibles existing on the Ethereum blockchain. Being a Cardslinger gives you access to some dope ass swag like exclusive LoC member benefits, NFT drops, utilities, and rad merch.

Community ownership is the name of the game. As a member, your task is to network with one another, create opportunities and help each other out.

Are you ready to begin a new world together?

  • Vision & Value

    We are the rebels of the NFT world. When others zig, we zag. Just because we can. We are a community of degen poker players that aren't shy about pushing the limits and redefinition of boundaries. This is bigger than our common love for the game. We want to build a legacy and create an icon for Web3.

    Community first: Without players, there is no poker. But it's not just a game, it's a lifestyle. Every experience in poker can be applied to real life and success. That's why our community is about gathering like-minded people to create, share and learn.

    Brand building: We want to build an entity that will last, our goal is not to make a few quick bucks but to create value and elevate the community.
  • COMMUNITY

    Our worldwide community is the heartbeat of our movement. Everyone is welcome to join and grow the club regardless of background and interest in poker. Our objective is to gather like-minded people and create a space for us to interact and empower other members with big ideas and the right tools for both poker and non-poker aspirations.

    We reward you for contributing to the community. Everybody who's anybody can play a role in building the brand together, opening up endless possibilities for the future. Ultimately, this community belongs to the people. You set the direction, we'll handle the rest.
  • WEB 3

    This is not a poker club. This is bigger than you and me.
    This is a lifestyle founded by the poker community.

    The LoC club is focused on building a brand that is beyond poker in which we aim to create an entire ecosystem around the LoC lore within Web3. It truly is an alternate reality.

    This allows our members to experience how our story unfolds and play a part in continuing the direction and narrative through gamification, artwork, the metaverse and more.
  • PHYSICAL WORLD

    Our interactions don't just start and stop in the digital world. We keep it real too.

    Collaborations: We are partnering up with a heavyweight organization in the poker industry. Announcing soon.

    Collectibles: Surprise, surprise! Rad poker and lifestyle merch coming soon.

    More cool shit: Something no NFT project has ever dared to do. We're not here to take part. We're here to take over.

    We are working towards building an empire on Web3 but the lines between the physical and digital worlds have begun to mend, making your NFT avatar more than just a token or a membership. It is your entire identity and functions as a key tool to access the community and its offerings, like interacting with other members, receiving drops and scoring utilities.
