MANIFESTO

14 February - Formerly known as Valentine`s Day in the old world. Then the proverbial shit hit the fan and today, it`s known as The Great Delist.

The pandemic went all kinds of fugazi. Vaccines weren`t cutting it anymore and the virus damn near wiped out the entire goddamn planet.

The rich and powerful joined forces, calling themselves, The Authority.

They funded biomechanical development known as Hodling. Which meant no more virus, no more sickness, Hell, you could even lose a limb like it ain`t no thing and just get a new one.. With a new lease on life, these D-gen donks drowned themselves in a sea of degeneracy.

That`s when it happened. The Authority traded the people`s hope for total global control and took everything from us. Even our playing cards. Imagine that. A poker player without cards but… it was “obey or be sent to The Pit”, Never to be seen again.

That got us tilted. From the darkness of oppression, the Cardslingers were born. A niche group of rebels who did whatever they wanted and that was to play mother lovin` poker.

So listen up, there's an urban legend… That an OG Cardslinger has discovered that poker’s rule of play holds the secret to taking down The Authority. Now, he is assembling a ragtag gang of Cardslingers to join his mission in deciphering the secret and using it to create a free decentralized place, built by its people, for the people.

Under the cloak of night, head over to The Alley and look for a small shop with trap doors. When the light above it glows "LIVE CARDS", that`s where you`ll find him.

Are you ready to join us? Welcome to the Legend of Cards.
